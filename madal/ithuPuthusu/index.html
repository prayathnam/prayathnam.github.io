Ragam: Durga 

			Manatuyarnīkki puṇaiyena nindru
			Mental worries – take away – like a boat – stay
			vinai pala nīkkum durgē
			obstacle – many take away – Durga
			(Taking away all the mental worries and obstacles, you are the refuge like a boat, O Durga!)

Ragam: Palini 

			Aŗŗātezum bayangaļai māŗŗiyē
			Unlimited-coming fears change

			Kūŗŗāi azittiţum koţuñcina Pālini nīyē
			Like-Yama crush angered Pālini you-are
			(With great anger, like Yama you crush and change all the unlimited fears, O Pālini!)

Ragam: Sivasakti 

			Eñciya pirvayil ezukinŗa tuyartanai
			Left-over birth ensuing agonies

			Añcēl enŗē aŗuļiţum Sivasakti
			‘fear-not’ bless Sivasakti
			(In the left over births, you bless and assure not to fear, O Sivasakti)

Ragam: kamalā manōhari 

			Puvi bōgam poruļ yōgam nalkiyē puvi vāzvai
			Worldly- pleasures monetary riches give worldly life

			Pōŗŗiţum kamalā manōhari
			Great kamalā manōhari
			(By granting (giving) worldly pleasures and riches, you make worldly life great, O kamalā manōhari)

Ragam: Amritavarshini 

			Alai kaţalin vaļar selvam nilaiperavē
			Wave-sea progressive wealth to eternally stay

			Alai ēŗiyum pāŗkaţalin amritavarshini
			Wave-generating milky ocean amritavarshini
			(Being born of the milky ocean, you make wealth (which is not permanent) stay eternally, O amritavarshini)

Ragam: Kanakāngi 

			Nidiyāki nitya porulāki uyir tokaiyin
			Wealth everyday stuff living beings

			Katiyākik kākkindŗa kanakāngi
			Refuge bless kanakāngi
			(Being both the invaluable wealth and everyday requirements of living beings, You bless and grant refuge, O kanakāngi)

Ragam: Saraswathi 

			Kalaiyāki kavinuru kāviyangal palavāgi
			As art poetry literature and the like

			Vilaiyillā mei arivin vadivāna saraswathi
			Invaluable Gnana form Saraswathi
			(Being art, poetry, literature and the like, you are also the form of invaluable realisation (Gnana), O Saraswathi

Ragam: Pūrnacandrikā 

			Poi aŗivin vēraruttu pollāta māsaŗuttu
			False-thought pull out from root bad cut off wrong

			Mei aŗivait tandiţum pūrnacandrikā
			True-knowledge grant pūrnacandrikā
			(Pulling out false thoughts from the root and cutting off bad, You grant real knowledge, O Pūrnacandrikā)

Ragam: Kalyāni 

			Nān mugan nāyakiyāi nāl vēda utporuļāi
			Four headed consort four vedas essence

			Nān enatennum serukkarukkum kalyāṇi
			Me & myself arrogance-demolish kalyāṇi
			Being the consort of the four headed (Brahma) and the essence of four Vedas, you demolish the arrogance of people, 
			Oh kalyāni
